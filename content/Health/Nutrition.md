Nutrition is the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.

## Core Principles

### Balanced Diet
- Variety of foods
- Adequate nutrients
- Portion control

### Macronutrients
- Carbohydrates
- Proteins
- Fats

### Micronutrients
- Vitamins
- Minerals
- Trace elements

## Application

1. **Diet Planning**
   - Meal preparation
   - Nutrient tracking
   - Dietary adjustments

2. **Health Goals**
   - Weight management
   - Energy levels
   - Disease prevention

## Best Practices
- Focus on whole foods
- Limit processed foods
- Stay hydrated
