Content Strategy involves planning, creating, and managing content to achieve business goals and engage target audiences.

## Core Components

### Audience Analysis

- Demographics
- Interests and needs
- Content preferences

### Content Planning

- Topic selection
- Format and channels
- Editorial calendar

### Performance Measurement

- Engagement metrics
- Conversion rates
- ROI analysis

## Application

1. **Marketing Campaigns**

   - Brand awareness
   - Lead generation
   - Customer retention

2. **Content Creation**
   - Blog posts
   - Social media
   - Video production

## Best Practices

- Align with goals
- Be consistent
- Measure and adjust
