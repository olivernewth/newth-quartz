The Compensation Calculator is a tool for calculating total compensation packages, including salary, benefits, and other incentives.

## Core Components

### Base Salary
- Annual or hourly rate
- Overtime considerations
- Pay frequency

### Benefits
- Health insurance
- Retirement contributions
- Paid time off

### Incentives
- Bonuses
- Stock options
- Profit sharing

## Application

1. **Job Offers**
   - Evaluating total value
   - Comparing opportunities
   - Making informed decisions

2. **HR Planning**
   - Budget forecasting
   - Compensation strategy
   - Employee satisfaction

## Best Practices
- Include all components
- Use accurate data
- Regularly review calculations
