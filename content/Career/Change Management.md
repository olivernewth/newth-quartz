Change Management involves guiding and supporting individuals and organizations through transitions to achieve desired outcomes.

## Core Components

### Communication

- Clear messaging
- Stakeholder engagement
- Feedback channels

### Training and Support

- Skill development
- Resource provision
- Ongoing assistance

### Monitoring and Evaluation

- Progress tracking
- Impact assessment
- Continuous improvement

## Application

1. **Organizational Change**

   - Process redesign
   - Cultural shifts
   - Technology adoption

2. **Leadership**
   - Vision setting
   - Team alignment
   - Conflict resolution

## Best Practices

- Be transparent
- Involve stakeholders
- Adapt to feedback
